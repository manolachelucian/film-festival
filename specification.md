# Festival Proposal: Prague GastroJazz 2026

## 1. Festival Identity
* **Name:** Prague GastroJazz Open 2026
* **Edition:** 5th Jubilee Edition
* **Location:** Střelecký Island, Prague (Shoot Island)
* **Date:** June 19 – 21, 2026 (Friday – Sunday)
* **Motto:** "Rhythm on the plate, taste in the ears."

## 2. Target Audience & Character
The festival targets lovers of premium gastronomy and Jazz/Soul/Funk music. It focuses on pairing experiences (e.g., a concert combined with wine tasting).

**Target Group:**
* Foodies and "fine street food" enthusiasts (age 25–55).
* Fans of Jazz, Swing, R&B, and Lo-Fi Hip Hop.
* Tourists seeking an authentic experience in the city center.

**Main Pillars:**
1.  **Music:** From classic Swing to modern Electro-Swing and Acid Jazz.
2.  **Gastro:** Live cooking shows, pop-up bistro stalls, wine tastings.

## 3. Web Structure (Sitemap)
The website will be divided into the following sections:

1.  **Home:**
    * Hero video (atmosphere).
    * Current Headliners (Top 3).
    * "Buy Tickets" CTA.
2.  **Program (Schedule):**
    * Interactive timeline (filter: Music / Food).
    * Divided by days (Fri, Sat, Sun).
3.  **Line-up (Performers & Chefs):**
    * Band profiles (bio, Spotify link).
    * Chef profiles (bio, cuisine type).
4.  **Venues & Map:**
    * Island map with highlighted zones (Main Stage, Taste Zone, Chill Park).
5.  **Info:**
    * Transport, Cashless system, Accessibility.

## 4. Venues
The festival takes place at three specific locations on the island:
* **Groove Stage (Main Stage):** Major concerts (Capacity: 2000).
* **Taste Lab (Gastro Tent):** Workshops, cooking shows, tastings (Capacity: 200).
* **River Lounge (Chill Zone):** DJ sets, smaller acoustic projects, seating on grass.
