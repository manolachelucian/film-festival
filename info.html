Practical Information

Location & Access

The festival takes place in the heart of Prague.

Střelecký Island (Střelecký ostrov)
110 00 Prague 1
Czech Republic

(Interactive map will be loaded here via API)

Venues

  • Groove Stage

    Main stage for big bands and headliners.

  • Taste Lab

    Covered area for cooking shows and tastings.

  • River Lounge

    Smaller stage by the water for DJs and soloists.

Tickets

We offer 1-day passes and full weekend tickets.

  • Basic Pass: Allows entry to music stages.
  • Gastro Pass: Includes 5 tasting tokens.
